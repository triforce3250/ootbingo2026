Ocarina of Time Bingo Tournament 2026

Tournament Schedule

Phase Dates Details
Seeding Races August 15th - 29th ESNB / SNB / Others. Best of first 2 times or Bingo Leaderboard time.
Group Phase August 30th - September 20th 3 weeks long, 1 match per week.
Single Elimination Bracket September 20th - October 18th/25th Top 16 (or Top 24 if there are 40+ entrants).

General Information

  • Sign up will be open until Saturday, August 29th, at 11:59pm US-Western (UTC-7). Sign up through the Bingo Tournament Discord.
  • The tournament will officially start on Sunday, August 30th. The tournament consists of 2 phases: a Groups phase lasting 3 weeks followed by a single-elimination bracket phase lasting 4-5 weeks. In the weeks before the tournament start, there will be seeding races used to determine the first round of the groups phase.
  • Entrants should plan on being available for one match a week. Not all entrants will qualify for the single-elimination bracket phase.
